The Shaughnessy Lab at Oklahoma State University investigates physiological and evolutionary mechanisms that shape ion transport, endocrine signaling, and thermal tolerance in fishes and amphibians. We integrate approaches from comparative physiology, molecular biology, and evolutionary endocrinology to understand how organisms respond and adapt to environmental challenges.
