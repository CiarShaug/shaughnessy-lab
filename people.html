PrincipalInvestigator

Ciaran A. Shaughnessy, Ph.D.

he/him

Assistant Professor of Integrative Physiology, Department of Integrative Biology, Oklahoma State University

Dr. Shaughnessy’s research bridges comparative, applied, and translational physiology. His lab investigates epithelial ion transport, endocrine signaling, and pharmacological modulation using bioinformatic, molecular, cellular, tissue, and whole-organism approaches. This work spans both evolutionary and biomedical contexts, from the diversification of endocrine and epithelial systems across vertebrates to the study of epithelial dysfunction in diseases like cystic fibrosis.
